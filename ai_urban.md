[The AI Revolution](https://waitbutwhy.com/2015/01/artificial-intelligence-revolution-1.html)
Tim Urban Jan 2015

TL;DR
Artificial Narrow intelligence will improve surprisingly quickly leading to Artificial General Intelligence and later Artificial Superintelligence. The arrival of ASI ought to be one of the chief concerns of humanity, for the result will either be immortality or extinction

Summary

The argument can be modeled as follows:
1. The typical person is blind to our rate of technological progress due to the Law of Accelerating Returns and is unfamiliar with the transition of ANI -> AGI -> ASI and AI Safety
2. ANI -> AGI will occur soon
	1. (Hardware) cps/$1000 is expected to grow exponentially
	2. (Software) There are multiple viable approaches such as brain emulation, genetic algorithms, and self-improving AI systems
3. ASI will have a quick takeoff after AGI is developed because of the advantages of hardware/software over wetware, and recursive self-improvement is viable
4. The arrival of ASI will lead to either the extinction or immortality of our species
5. The alignment problem is difficult, and due to the likelihood of a singleton, we will need to get this right the first time
